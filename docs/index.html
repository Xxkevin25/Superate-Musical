The musical Supérate is a magnificent work inspired by the moments lived at the Centro Superate Fundacion Alberto Motta by the students of the class of 2022. The musical reflects situations that show what life is like for a Supérate student with all the challenges that come with accepting this opportunity given to children with enormous academic potential. But not everything is about a study program in this musical are included the desire and commitment that has the superate cast to form better people for society all this through a vision of education different from that already existing in the country and thus give examples of life as raising the bar, give the extra mile and return the knowledge with new generations.
